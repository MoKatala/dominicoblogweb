Who is the perfect customer?

Published 3 months ago5 min read4 comments

To be honest, there is no difference between what you see in the screenshot above and what happens in business. Many of us waste time trying to convert potential clients that aren't potential in the first place. Think about it - how can you sell brown sugar to a person who is diabetic? How do you even plan to sell condoms to a priest? Where did you get that audacity to market pork chops to Muslims? You will save yourself a lot of hustle if only you focus on converting potentials that really need the product or service you are offering. And, you dont have to go to business school to learn this. Just ask them if they are willing to pay. If not, they are not the one. Go find the one.
